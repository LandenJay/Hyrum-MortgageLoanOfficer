I'm a Mortgage Loan Officer

Hello Everybody! I'm Hyrum, a dedicated mortgage loan officer at Bedrock Home Loans, and my goal is to guide you through the home loan process. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer or have experience in the market, I work tirelessly to help you find the right loan for your needs. We love teaching you how to use credit and improve your credit score! We have our own private down payment programs and access to state and federal programs. I would love to meet with you and help you on your homeownership journey.
